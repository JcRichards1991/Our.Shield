Backoffice Access

Backoffice Access gives you the ability to configure the backoffice access url - by default & out-of-the-box, it's /umbraco/ - Allows you to restrict your desired backoffice access url via a white-list of IP Address(es) and rewrite or redirect unauthorised access to a location of your choice!

Note: Disabling Backoffice Access will reset the access url back to being "/umbraco"
