Backoffice Access

Backoffice Access gives you the ability to configure the backoffice access url - by default & out-of-the-box, it's /umbraco/ - Allows you to restrict your desired backoffice access url via a white-list of IP Address(es) and rewrite or redirect unauthorised access to a location of your choice via:

  • Static string - i.e. "/404-not-found"
  • XPath expression - i.e. "/root/homepage/standardPage[@isDoc AND @nodeName = '404 Not Found']"
    OR
  • Using a content picker

Note: Disabling Backoffice Access will reset the access url back to being "/umbraco"
